What is Marta?

"Formed in 1971 as strictly a bus system, MARTA operates a network of bus routes linked to a rapid transit system consisting of 48 miles (77 km) of rail track with 38 Subway stations. MARTA's rapid transit system is the eighth-largest rapid transit system in the United States by ridership." (Wikipedia)

Some Fun Facts

  • MARTA stands for the Metropolitan Atlanta Rapid Transit Authority.
  • MARTA's station names are quite unique and reflect the local culture and landmarks. (The Dome/GWCC/Philips Arena/CNN Center)
  • MARTA's rail system is known for having a significant portion of its rail lines underground, which is revatively rare in the US.
  • MARTA keeps over 185,000 cars off the road every day.
  • Many stations feature public art installations, including sculptures, murals, and other works of art.
